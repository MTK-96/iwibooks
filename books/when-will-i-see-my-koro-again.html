Details

When Tama's Koro passed away, Tama was very sad and missed him dearly. One day, Tama wanted to see his Koro, so he went to his friends the farm animals and asked, "When will I see my Koro again?" With the help of his friends, the farm animals, his dog Kuri, and an eel, Tama is eventually guided up the Great Hill to visit a wise old Kauri tree, who could help him. This beautifully illustrated, heart-warming picture book explores themes of grief, friendship, adventure, and remembering loved ones who have passed.
