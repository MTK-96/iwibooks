our story

Indigenous stories have been told and shared through many generations. Often received in dreams and moments of clarity.

Our forefathers were inspired by these dreams and with sacrifice and courage, forged a new life in a land undiscovered.

As generations past, many stopped hearing, stopped dreaming, stopped believing, stopped understanding.

Our ancestors have emerged once again, to reconnect with those who will hear and share their messages of courage, strength and inspiration.

Iwibooks does not represent traditional indigenous tales but embraces new indigenous stories depicting connection, remembrance, courage, strength, inspiration, community and family.
