Returns & Replacements

Returns or replacements will not be accepted 30 days after the invoice date. Products cannot be returned or replaced without prior authorization. Please send an email with a list of the items to be returned, your invoice number and the reason for the return.

If the item purchased has not been opened, used or damaged, we will return the cost of product however we cannot return the cost of shipping or postage. Items returned must be eligible for resale. We do not sell used or second hand products.

The return time may take up to 4 weeks from the approved returned date. Please allow time to:

  1. Receive your returned products (3-5 business days)
  2. Process your return (3-5 business days)
  3. Our financial provider to process the refund request. (5-10 business days)

Refunds for orders placed online or by phone are credited back to the original credit card or method in which the order was purchased. We do not send, receive or refund cash payments.
